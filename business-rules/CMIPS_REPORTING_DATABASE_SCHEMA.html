üîÑ Data Flow Architecture

  • Source Systems: C√∫ram (case management operational DB), CGI Advantage (payroll operational DB), ESP (Electronic Service Portal)
  • ETL Process: Nightly extraction (11 PM - 6 AM) transforms and loads data into RD_* tables
  • Schema: All reporting tables use prefix "RD_" and reside in BOWARE schema
  • Purpose: Reporting, analytics, compliance, data retention, online archive for purged operational data
  • Retention: Operational data (active) + Online Archive (5.5-7.5 years) = Total 7.5 years minimum
