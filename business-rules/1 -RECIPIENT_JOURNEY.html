Recipient Journey

From initial contact to hiring a provider

Overview

This guide walks you through the complete journey as an IHSS recipient, from your first contact with the county to successfully hiring and managing a provider.

Phase 1

Initial Application (County Handles)

Step 1: Contact County

  • Phone, walk-in, or referral
  • A county worker takes the information

Step 2: County Worker Creates Referral/Application in CMIPS

  • If someone else referred you: County creates a Referral (Person Type = OPEN_REFERRAL)
  • If you applied yourself: County creates an Application (Person Type = APPLICANT)

Step 3: County Processes Your Case

  • CIN clearance and Medi-Cal verification
  • Service eligibility assessment
  • Health Care Certification (SOC 873)
  • Case approval/denial

Step 4: Case Approved

  • Case Status = ELIGIBLE or PRESUMPTIVE_ELIGIBLE
  • Person Type = RECIPIENT
  • You can now register for ESP
Phase 2

ESP Self-Service Registration (You Do This)

Step 1: Go to ESP Website

  • URL: https://www.etimesheets.ihss.ca.gov
  • Click "Register as New User"

Step 2: Complete 5-Step Registration

Step 2.1: Validate Identity

  • Select "I am a Recipient"
  • Enter Case Number (provided by county)
  • Enter Last 4 digits of CIN (Client Index Number)
  • Enter Last Name, First Name, DOB
  • System validates against CMIPS records

Step 2.2: Verify Email

  • Enter email address (twice)
  • System sends 6-digit verification code
  • Enter code to verify

Step 2.3: Create Username

  • 6-20 characters
  • Letters, numbers, underscore, hyphen only
  • Must be unique

Step 2.4: Create Password

  • Minimum 8 characters
  • Must include: uppercase, lowercase, number, special character
  • Strength indicator shown

Step 2.5: Security Questions

  • Select 3 different security questions
  • Provide answers (for password recovery)

Step 3: Registration Complete

  • Welcome email sent
  • You can now log in to ESP
Phase 3

Hiring a Provider (You Can Do This in ESP)

Prerequisites

  • Age 18 or older
  • Case Status = ELIGIBLE or PRESUMPTIVE_ELIGIBLE
  • Registered in ESP

Step 1: Log in to ESP

  • Go to https://www.etimesheets.ihss.ca.gov
  • Use your username and password

Step 2: Access "Hire Provider"

From Recipient Home → "Hire Provider"

Step 3: Search for Provider

Search Options:

  • Provider Name (Last Name required, First Name optional)
  • Provider Number (9 digits)
  • Last 4 digits of SSN

Optional Filters:

  • County (defaults to yours)
  • Gender
  • Language

Click "Search"

Step 4: Review Search Results

Results Show:

  • Provider Name
  • Provider Number
  • Gender
  • County
  • Languages
  • Status (Available, Already Hired, Not Eligible, Restricted)

Status Meanings:

Status Meaning
Available Can be hired
Already Hired Already on your case
Not Eligible Cannot be hired (see details)
Restricted Has CORI restrictions

Step 5: Select and Confirm Provider

Click "Select" on a provider

Review Provider Information:

  • Provider details
  • Eligibility status (Background Check, Orientation, SOC 846)
  • Current assignments (how many active cases)

Before Hiring:

  • Interview or speak with provider
  • Verify availability for your schedule
  • Discuss services needed
  • Confirm comfort with your needs
  • Check references if desired

Your Social Worker can help with interviews and verifying qualifications.

Step 6: Confirm Hire

Review:

  • Provider name and number
  • Your case information
  • Authorized services
  • Monthly hours

Certification:

  • I am age 18 or older
  • I want to hire this provider
  • Provider will be assigned immediately
  • I will direct the provider's work
  • I will review and approve timesheets
  • Enter your name as electronic signature
  • Check "I agree and want to hire"
  • Click "Complete Hire"

Step 7: Hire Confirmation

Success Message:

  • Confirmation number
  • Provider assigned
  • What happens next

What Happens Next:

Immediate Assignment

  • Provider is assigned to your case
  • Can begin working right away

County Processing (1-2 Business Days)

  • Social Worker notified
  • Provider contacted
  • Pay rate set
  • Hours assigned
  • Timesheets generated

Your Next Steps

  • Contact provider to coordinate schedule
  • Discuss care needs
  • Agree on work schedule

Timesheet Setup

  • Generated for next pay period
  • Provider submits electronically
  • You review and approve in ESP

Notifications

  • You receive confirmation email
  • Provider receives assignment notification
  • County receives processing alert

Restrictions and Limitations

You Cannot Hire a Provider If:

  • Under age 18 (contact Social Worker instead)
  • Case Status is not ELIGIBLE or PRESUMPTIVE_ELIGIBLE
  • Case is terminated or on leave
  • You've reached maximum providers for your case
  • Provider has CORI restrictions without proper waivers
  • Provider is not eligible to work

Help Desk

  • Can assist with search
  • Cannot complete the hire for you
  • For technical issues only

Two Pathways Summary

Pathway A: County Assigns Provider (Traditional)

  1. County worker assigns provider through CMIPS
  2. Provider receives notification
  3. Provider starts serving you
  4. You review timesheets

Pathway B: You Hire Provider Yourself (Self-Service)

  1. You register in ESP (after case is eligible)
  2. You search for providers
  3. You hire provider through ESP
  4. System notifies county
  5. County completes setup (1-2 business days)
  6. Provider starts serving you
  7. You review timesheets in ESP

Important Points

  • You cannot create your own case. County workers create it after initial contact.
  • You can register for ESP only after your case is approved (ELIGIBLE status).
  • You can hire providers yourself through ESP if you are 18+.
  • County still finalizes setup (pay rate, hours, timesheet generation) after your hire.
  • You direct the provider's work and approve/reject timesheets.

Summary Workflow

YOUR JOURNEY AS A RECIPIENT:

1. CONTACT COUNTY (Phone/Walk-in)
   ↓
2. COUNTY CREATES REFERRAL/APPLICATION
   ↓
3. COUNTY PROCESSES CASE (Assessment, Approval)
   ↓
4. CASE APPROVED → Status: ELIGIBLE
   ↓
5. YOU REGISTER IN ESP (Self-Service - 5 Steps)
   ↓
6. YOU LOGIN TO ESP
   ↓
7. YOU HIRE PROVIDER (Self-Service - Search & Hire)
   ↓
8. COUNTY COMPLETES SETUP (1-2 days)
   ↓
9. PROVIDER STARTS SERVING YOU
   ↓
10. YOU REVIEW/APPROVE TIMESHEETS IN ESP

TL;DR

You don't enroll yourself initially — the county creates your case. Once approved, you can register in ESP and hire providers yourself if you're 18+.
